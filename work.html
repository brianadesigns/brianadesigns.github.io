Musician's Friend is a California-based music warehouse. The billboard was inspired by my guitar technical illustration I made in Adobe: Illustrator. The images below show off the wireframe for my guitar illustration. Below that are screenshots of applying the guitar onto a billboard layout using Adobe: InDesign, accompanied by a mockup done in Adobe: Photoshop.

The Black Cat Market is a Pittsburgh-based coffee shop that allows you to play with cats as you enjoy your beverage. The campaign below features a stationery set made in Adobe: Illustrator, a corporate standards manual made in Adobe: Indesign, and a flyer made in Adobe: Illustrator. The logo featured in these pieces was also redesigned using Illustrator.

Don's Pizzeria is a small Pittsburgh-based, family-owned shop. This project started off as a rough sketch which eventually turned into an Adobe: Illustrator-made menu. The Don's Pizzeria logo is based off of the logo from the website, the menu elements were all created in Adobe: Illustrator.

AHR is a company responsible for producing helmets and helmet accessories for your everyday motor-bike riding and more. This campaign in particular features a catalog containing all of their helmets sorted by catagory, a die-cut mailer in the shape of one of their helmets, and a tradeshow display piece. Adobe: Illustrator was used to re-create the AHR logo and to layout the die-cut mailer. Adobe: Photoshop was used to make the entirety of the tradeshow piece, as well as to edit and crop out the backgrounds on the helmets featured in the catalog and the helmet used for the mailer. Adobe: InDesign was used to layout the catalog.

Sharp is a company specializing in a variety of electronics, including printers. This piece showcases a magazine ad for Sharp's printer, the MX-3571S. This piece was made in Adobe: InDesign, with elements such as the 'Need A New Printer?' title and the CMY ink splatter made in Adobe: Photoshop. The image of the printer was also edited and cut out of its original image using Photoshop.
